Expenses Sheet

Just another app to register expenses.
But this one use a Google Sheet as Database, stored in your Google Drive and only you and who you want have access to it.

How it Works

This is an android app that allows you to insert expenses into a Google Spreadsheet. that facilitates. This sheet can be individual or shared among several people.

In order to create, read, write and store Google Spreadsheets, the app needs Google Drive permissions.

Privacy Policy

We respect user privacy, so we do not collect or store any account information or data inserted in any spreadsheets or other Google Drive file.

Data Security

The data is stored exclusively in the Google Spreadsheet and created privately. Security is ensured by Google Drive's security policies.

Disclosure

We are not responsible for any information that is made public from the Spreadsheets created by the application. The responsibility and owner of the sheet belongs to the person who created the sheet.

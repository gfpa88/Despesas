Expenses Sheet

Just another app to register expenses.
But this one use a Google Sheet as Database, stored in your Google Drive and only you and who you want have access to it.

  • How it Works

How it Works

This is an android app that allows you to insert expenses into a Google Spreadsheet. This sheet can be individual or shared among several people. You can found it in the route of your Google Drive or in the Google Spreadsheet home screen.

In order to create, read, write and store Google Spreadsheets, the app needs Google Drive and Google Spreadsheets permissions.
